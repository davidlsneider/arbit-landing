The proof

A decision you can read.

Most protection asks for faith in a process you never see. Arbit produces a written decision that shows its reasoning — signed by the human who made it.

Behind the mark, every protected purchase carries its own record: the protected amount, the maximum remedy, the claim window, and what the purchase is for. The mark never appears on a purchase that is not protected.

Sophisticated deals

Terms people and agents can work with.

For a straightforward purchase, Arbit can supply standard terms. For a more sophisticated deal, either side—or an agent acting for them—can propose terms, inspect the current version, or bring an existing contract for a supportability review. Arbit responds with what it can support and what must change before protection can be offered.
